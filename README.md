Project Details

Problem Statement: 
Tirana provides a variety of sports facilities, such as gyms, football fields, basketball courts, and tennis courts, to encourage students to stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle. However, despite having these resources, students often face significant challenges when trying to access and use them efficiently.
In many cases, facility availability is not clearly communicated. Reservation processes are often handled manually through administrative staff, paper-based systems, or informal channels like messaging apps and group chats. These outdated methods are not only time-consuming but also prone to errors and miscommunication.
As a result, several common problems come up:
•	Double bookings: Multiple students or groups may reserve the same facility at the same time due to lack of a centralized system.
•	Confusion about availability: Students cannot easily see which time slots are free or already booked, leading to uncertainty and frustration.
•	Difficulty managing reservations: Changing or canceling bookings can be complicated, often requiring direct communication with staff.
•	Lack of organized payment or membership tracking: There is often no clear system for handling payments, tracking memberships, or verifying who is eligible to use certain facilities.
•	Inefficient use of resources: Facilities may remain underused at certain times while being overcrowded at others due to poor scheduling visibility.

Proposed solution:
The Sports Facility Booking System addresses these issues by introducing a centralized digital platform. Through this system, students can:
•	View real-time availability of all sports facilities.
•	Make, modify, or cancel reservations from any device.
•	Receive confirmations and reminders for their bookings.
•	Access clear information about rules, pricing, and facility usage.
•	Ensure fair and organized access for all users.
By digitizing and automating the booking process, the system reduces confusion, minimizes scheduling conflicts, and significantly improves the overall user experience for both students and university staff.

Project Scope:
Aim:
To design and develop a centralized digital system that simplifies the booking and management of sports facilities in Tirana.
Objectives:
•	Develop a user-friendly interface for browsing and booking facilities.
•	Implement real-time availability tracking.
•	Enable users to manage their reservations.
•	Provide an admin dashboard for facility and user management.
•	Ensure secure authentication and data handling.
•	Integrate optional payment and membership features.
•	Improve overall efficiency and reduce booking conflicts.

Application Description:
The Sports Facility Booking System is a system designed to simplify the management and reservation of sports facilities. It acts as a centralized platform where all information related to sports venues, such as availability, schedules, bookings, and user access, is organized in one place.
The application replaces manual and inefficient booking methods with a digital solution that is fast, transparent, and easy to use. People and staff can access the system anytime and from anywhere, allowing them to plan their activities without unnecessary delays or confusion.
Additionally, the system helps administrators manage facilities more effectively by providing tools to monitor usage, handle reservations, and maintain organized records.

Users of the System
The application is designed to serve multiple types of users, each with different roles and permissions:

1. Citizens
Tirana residents are the primary users of the system. They use the application to:
•	Browse available sports facilities (gym, football field, basketball court etc.).
•	Check real-time availability of time slots.
•	Book, modify, or cancel reservations.
•	Receive notifications or confirmations for bookings.
•	View their booking history.

2. Administrators
Administrators are responsible for managing the system and ensuring smooth operation. Their tasks include:
•	Managing facility schedules and availability.
•	Approving or monitoring reservations (if required).
•	Handling user accounts and permissions.
•	Tracking usage statistics and reports.
•	Managing pricing, memberships, or access rules.

3. Facility Staff 
In some cases, staff members may also use the system to:
•	Verify bookings on-site.
•	Assist users with reservations.
•	Report maintenance issues or unavailable facilities.
•	Monitor daily schedules.

Key Features
The Sports Facility Booking System includes several important features that improve efficiency and user experience:

1. Real-Time Availability
Users can view up-to-date schedules for all facilities, ensuring they only book available time slots and avoid conflicts.

2. Online Booking System
Users can easily reserve facilities through the application without needing to contact staff manually.

3. Booking Management
Users can:
•	Modify existing reservations.
•	Cancel bookings if plans change.
•	View their past and upcoming bookings.

4. User Authentication
Secure login and account management ensure that only authorized users can access and book facilities.

5. Notifications and Reminders
The system can send alerts or confirmations via email or in-app notifications to keep users informed about their bookings.

6. Admin Dashboard
Administrators have access to a control panel where they can:
•	Manage facilities and schedules.
•	Monitor usage.
•	Generate reports.

7. Payment and Membership Management 
If required, the system can support:
•	Online payments for bookings.
•	Membership plans.
•	Access control based on user status.
